<<HDMI output>>

::Digital Video::
Analog Devices ADV7511 HDMI Transmitter provides digital video interface
*225 MHz transmitter is HDMI 1.4 and DVI 1.0 compatible
*1080p60 with 16-bit YCbCr, 4:2:2 mode color


::Digital Audio::
ADV7511 supports S/PDIF (*and 8-channel I2S audio **not connected**)
